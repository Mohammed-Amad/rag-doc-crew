# How many employees does Solvane have, and does that headcount growth match what's reflected in Q3 operating expenses?
## Executive Summary
Solvane Dynamics Inc. has a total of **176** employees as of the end of Q3 of the current fiscal year. The key points supporting this information and the reflection of headcount growth in Q3 operating expenses include:
* The company experienced a headcount growth from **164** at the end of Q2 to **176** at the end of Q3, representing an increase of **12** employees.
* This growth is reflected in the Q3 operating expenses, with total personnel costs representing approximately **68%** of total OpEx in Q3.
