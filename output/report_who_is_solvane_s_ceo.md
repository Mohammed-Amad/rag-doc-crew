# Who is Solvane's CEO?
## Executive Summary
The available documents do not mention **Solvane's CEO**. However, key facts about Solvane include:
* **Solvane Dynamics Inc.** designs autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) and fleet-orchestration software for third-party logistics warehouses.
* The company was founded in **2019** and is headquartered in **Denver, Colorado**, with an engineering and hardware validation site in **Krakow, Poland**.
* As of the end of Q3 of the current fiscal year, Solvane employs **176 people**.
* The mission of Solvane is to make warehouse fulfillment faster and safer without displacing the workforce.
* Solvane Dynamics closed Q3 of the current fiscal year with total revenue of **$5.14M**, up from **$4.36M** in Q2, representing **17.9%** quarter-over-quarter growth.
* Growth was driven primarily by Priority-tier and Enterprise-tier FleetOS expansions at existing 3PL customers.
* Operating margin improved to **9.7%** from **3.1%** in the prior quarter.
* Solvane served **11 active FleetOS customers** across **34 warehouse sites** at the end of Q3, up from **9 customers** and **27 sites** at the end of Q2.
* Net revenue retention (NRR) for Q3 was **121%**, compared to **112%** in Q2.
